I love playing football because I am quite good at playing it if I do say so myself. It is quite fun to kick the ball around and score. I play the position of a midfielder. Its job is to run up and down the field to pass to the striker which scores the goal. The midfielder also can score when the opportunity presents itself. The midfielder is a tiring position to play. If the midfielder loses the ball the ball goes to the defender. The job of the defender is to restrict the other teams midfielders and strikers from scoring.
